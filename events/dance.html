Jalsa Group Dance(Jashan)

Jalsa Group Dance (Jashan)

Rules and Regulations:


  • For Preliminary (Online)
  1. The video should not exceed 5 minutes.
  2. Usage of props in the video are allowed.
  3. The video should be single-shot and not be edited at all.
  4. No Bollywood/fusion songs will be entertained, pure folks songs should be used.
  5. Last date to register/upload the video for prelims round is February 11.
  6. Only one team per college is allowed in this event.
  7. Maximum of 8 members are allowed in one team.
  • For Finals
  1. The video should not exceed 10 minutes.
  2. All props are allowed except for the following :
    Any sharp objects ,fire , fog, smoke helium baloons, special lightning.
  3. No Bollywood /fusion songs will be entertained, pure folks songs should be used.
  4. Maximum of 8 members are allowed in one team along with 5 accompaniments.
  • Judging Criteria
  1. Choreography, Musicality, Expression, Creativity, Costume and makeup , Energy , Stage Coverage, and overall impact.
