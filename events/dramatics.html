QIRDAAR
QIRDAAR : Monologue is a prolonged talk or discourse by a single speaker, especially one dominating or monopolizing a conversation. NAVRANG presents QIRDAAR - MONOLOGUE COMPETITION - A competition that gives you the wheel, to flow into the direction of creativity and empowers your words. So lets unleash the actor inside you and know how much of a dominating character can you be.

Rules and Regulations:

  1. The performance must not exceed 5 minutes.
  2. Monologues from any movie or web series are not allowed. Although you can take it from any play or it can be self written.
  3. Use of Hindi or English or a mixture of both is allowed. However, the participant can use different accents and slangs to enhance their performance.
  4. Multiple entries from the same college are allowed.
  5. Vulgarity in any form of meaning and use of sexist, racist, casteist, homophobic or transphobic remarks shall lead to immediate disqualification of the participant.
  6. Inflammable substances are not allowed on the stage.
  7. Costumes and props are to be arranged by the participants. However, we can provide you with 2 chairs and a table at most.
  8. Music if needed, should be prepared before hand and given to the organizers in a standard secondary storage device.
  9. The participant must bring their college ID Card.
  10. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

ISHTEHAAR
NAVRANG- The Stage Play Society of P.G.D.A.V. announces its glorious competition "AD MAD - ISHTEHAAR in the annual fest ABHIVYAKTI’23. Show what you've got in there , the skills to make the act go wild and free. Lets put on the table the convincing power you hold , it's finally time to not hold your breath and just go with the flow... Time to make all the minds go mad with your remarkable ads.

Rules and Regulations:

  1. The topics for the AD MAD show will be given on the spot.
  2. Each team will have one hour for the preparation.
  3. The team should consist a maximum of 3 members.
  4. Maximum time limit of the performance is 1-4 minutes.
  5. Vulgarity in any form of meaning and use of sexist, racist, casteist, homophobic or transphobic remarks shall lead to immediate disqualification of the team.
  6. The participants must bring their college ID Card and team list.
  7. Criteria for judgement-
    • Content – Information, awareness and humour
    • Spontaneity/ expressions/acting/ fluency
    • Appeal of the advertisement
    • Overall script and play
    • Decision of judges will be final and binding.
  8. Teams must report by 10:45 AM in the Old Seminar Hall.
  9. Their product and slot will be given at 11:00 AM sharp.

RANG BAZAR
You ever wondered how to have fun while showcasing your talent , making people cheer for you and winning exciting prizes for the same? Here is a chance for that once in a lifetime opportunity . The Stage society of PGDAV - NAVRANG announces a STAGE PLAY COMPETITION , a competition that will let you play a character you choose.

Rules and Regulations:

  • PRELIMINIARY ROUND
    1. The teams have to send a 10-15 minutes video for the selection of finals.
    2. The video should be recorded in a single take, free of any cuts and retakes.
    3. Teams have to send a PDF of the script and synopsis.
    4. Vulgarity in any form of meaning and use of sexist, racist, casteist, homophobic or transphobic remarks shall lead to immediate disqualification of the participant.
  • FINAL ROUND
    1. The team should consist of a minimum of 7 members (onstage + backstage).
    2. The time limit For the final performance is 40 + 5 minutes from empty to empty stage.
    3. The team exceeding the time limit shall face negative marking.
    4. Use of Hindi or English or a mixture of both is allowed. However, the participant can use different accents and slangs to enhance their performance.
    5. The participants must bring their College ID Card and team list.
    6. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
