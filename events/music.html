alaap

Rules and Regulations:


General Guidelines:

  1. This is a solo singing competition with 2 rounds - Prelims and Finale.
  2. The event is open to both UG and PG students from all over India. Max age limit is 23 years, as of 31st January 2023.
  3. Only 4 registrations are allowed from each college in Prelims. In case of more than 4 registrations, they will be chosen on a first-come-first-serve basis.
  4. Only 2 registrations will be allowed from each college in Finale. They will be selected on the basis of the points marks provided by the judge(s).
  5. Participants/accompanists must carry their college ID cards.
  6. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Performance Guidelines:

  1. For prelims, the participants will have to submit their recorded performance along with their registrations on Google Form.
  2. The time limit for the prelim submission is 4 minutes.
  3. Only ghazals, folk, Sufi, and semi-classical songs or compositions are allowed.
  4. Backing tracks, karaoke, and accompanying instruments are allowed. The participants can play the accompanying instrument or ask a friend for live accompaniment. A maximum of one accompanist is allowed in the case of live instrument accompaniment.
  5. The audio of the uploaded video should be clear with no noise or disturbances in the background. If the committee identifies any kind of manipulation with the audio and/or video, the entry will be disqualified without any notice or negotiation.

Fantasia

Rules and Regulations:


Rules for Prelims (Online):


  1. Backing tracks/instrumentals are allowed.
  2. Accompanists and accompanying instruments are not allowed.
  3. The maximum duration of the performance is 4 minutes.
  4. The video should be completely raw and unedited, with no special effects or edits. Doing this will lead to disqualification.
  5. The songs should be in English language (Western). Rap and explicit songs are not allowed.
  6. Participants need to give a brief introduction of themselves in the beginning of the video before they start singing (name, college etc.) Failing to do this will lead to disqualification.
  7. The deadline for the first round is 11th February 23, 11:59pm.
  8. The results will be announced on the official Instagram Handle of Conundrum (conundrum_wms).

RULES FOR FINALS (Offline):


  1. The final round will be an offline event that will be held on the college campus.
  2. Backing tracks/instrumentals are not allowed.
  3. Maximum of ONE accompanist is allowed.
  4. Maximum duration of the performance is 6 minutes including the sound check and introduction of the participant.
  5. The performance will be judged on the basis of song selection, dynamics, pitch and overall impact.
  6. All participants must carry a valid identity card (Aadhar, PAN Card, Driving License, etc.)
  7. Decision of the judges will be final and binding on all the participants.
  8. Final event will take place on 14th February, 2023.
